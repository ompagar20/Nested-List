Engineering Domains

  1. Civil Engineering

    Civil engineering is a broad field that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of the built environment. It includes subfields such as:

    • Structural Engineering
    • Transportation Engineering
    • Environmental Engineering
    • Geotechnical Engineering

  2. Mechanical Engineering

    Mechanical engineering is a field that deals with the design, analysis, and manufacturing of machines and mechanical systems. It includes subfields such as:

    1. Thermal Engineering
    2. Manufacturing Engineering
    3. Mechatronics Engineering

  3. Electrical Engineering

    Electrical engineering is a field that deals with the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. It includes subfields such as:

    • Power Engineering
    • Control Systems Engineering
    • Electronics Engineering

  4. Chemical Engineering

    Chemical engineering is a field that deals with the design, development, and optimization of chemical processes and systems. It involves the use of chemistry, physics, and mathematics to solve problems in the production of chemicals, fuels, and other products.

  5. Computer Engineering

    Computer engineering is a field that deals with the design, development, and application of computer systems and networks. It includes subfields such as:

    • Hardware Engineering
    • Software Engineering
    • Computer Networks Engineering
